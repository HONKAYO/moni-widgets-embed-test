FWA - house economics

Will anyone want to fund the house?

In FWA, the NFT price is only half the problem. The harder part is how much ETH someone will lock behind it. Set the NFT value, choose the backing multiple, and watch where the house trade starts to work or break.

Controls

NFT reference value1.00 ETH
Log scale from tiny NFTs to blue-chip inventory. This is the value anchor for the model.
Backing multiple1.00x = 1.00 ETH
Backing is measured relative to the NFT. A 1x Punk is very different from a 1x cheap mint.
Assumed pool activity14 acq/day
The missing market variable. Until purchases begin, this is an assumption, not live throughput.
Pool crowding24 rest-weight
More competing inventory sharing the same fee flow.

What is fixed

Protocol facts
Backing sets inverse selection weight. Default acquisition surcharge is 10%. Fee split: 94% depositors, 5% crown, 1% protocol. Default bid payout is 85% of backing.

Model choices
NFT value, activity, crowding, relist friction, and reward support are assumptions. July 1 status: deposits are visible, protocol purchases have not started.
